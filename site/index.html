New Job Board

News Feed - Source :
[Open Source Hong Kong - New Job Board](http://opensource.hk/node/1060)

Open Source Hong Kong promotes job openings from companies, which open source skill is one of job requirement of these job opening. Job applicants can apply to employer directly, and currently this job board is free of chrage services for potential candidates and OSHK supporters.

New Job Board Service is now available at job.opensource.hk. Job Board Software is developed by Sammy Fung and released as free and open source on GitHub. If you like this job board software or look for support service of the software, please contact with author.
